About the photographer

The Eye
Behind It

I am a photographer driven by the quiet drama of everyday light — the way it falls through a window, bends around a face, or dissolves into a horizon. My work spans editorial portraiture, documentary landscapes, and intimate lifestyle stories.

Based in Chicago, Illinois, I've worked with publications, brands, and individuals who believe great photography is not just technique — it's a point of view.
